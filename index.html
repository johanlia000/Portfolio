Hi! I'm Lia. I'm an aspiring, creative engineer who wants to be at the center of the technology field. I value the interdisciplinary and creative nature of computing and design. It’s where my passion resides and why I'm a Human Centered Design and Engineering major at the University of Washington. I am also excited about computer science and am concentrating in data science and practicing coding with internships, projects, and courses. My goal is to bring designs to life through coding.

I naturally combine the logical and algorithmic approaches of engineering with the more fluid and creative methods of design thinking. My journey has validated what I first discovered in high school as the lead software developer of the robotics team and as an intern promoting technology throughout my city. I enjoy the camaraderie and dynamics of teamwork, and the challenges of designing, coding and implementing technology products. I also don’t mind finding myself outside of my comfort zone, which is a natural result of continuous learning. Now, I’m growing my leadership skills and exploring product design and development through coursework, research, and extracurricular activities.
