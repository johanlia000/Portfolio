About me.









Hi! I'm Lia. I'm an aspiring creative engineer studying Human Centered Design and Engineering with a concentration in Data Science at the University of Washington. I am also excited to integrate computer science within my design practice. It’s where my passion resides! My goal is to bring designs to life through coding.

I naturally combine the logical and algorithmic approaches of engineering with the more fluid and creative methods of design thinking. I enjoy the camaraderie and dynamics of teamwork and the challenges of designing, coding, and implementing technology products. I also don’t mind finding myself outside of my comfort zone, which is a natural result of continuous learning.

I'm actively growing my leadership skills and exploring product design and development through data science research, technology internships, and involvement with Design For America UW, where I'm the current finance officer.
