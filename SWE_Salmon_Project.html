SALMON FUN RUN

Context: Society of Women Engineerings (SWE) Hackathon 2019


Duration: 9 hours


Collaboration: Member on 4-person team


Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Figma

I was 1 of 4 on a team creating, designing, and competing in the Society of Women Engineer's hackathon at the University of Washington. Our interdiscplinary team consisted of Human Centered and Engineering, Computer Science, and Informatics majors. This year my team focused on their prompt stating that "Social good is about using the potential of individuals, technology, and collaboration to create positive societal impact."

My team focused on educating and helping combat the environmental decline in the puget sound region that directly affects salmon populations. Alongisde helping raise awareness of the Pacific North West ecosystem and environment, we focused on creating a sense of community through a salmon fun run.
