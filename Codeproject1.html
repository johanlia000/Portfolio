About our project

Our web application helps high schoolers easily view college statistcs to help them choose the colleges they want to apply to and/or attend. My group worked with a dataset from the U.S Department of Education that focused on admission statistics from different colleges in the United States. The data was from the most recent years (past 20 years) showing admission rates, tuition rates, SAT/ACT scores, and more. Our primary audiences were high school and college transfer students wanting to apply to college, and our secondary audiences were the applicants' parents. We narrowed down our dataset based on key information students would want to know such as location, diversity, test scores, and admission statistics.
