Scrapbook Stickers

Context: Personal project


Duration: Four weeks


Collaboration: Individual


Tools: Adobe Illustrator

I challenged myself to create a digital scrapbook of customized stickers as a way to expand my design skills.

ICON DESIGNS

I've always loved creating scrapbooks to document memories and had just completed a design class where I learned Adobe Illustrator. As a designer, I wanted to get more comfortable with Illustrator's pen tool, so I designed a scrapbook and created fun, colorful stickers that improved my pen tool skills.

I found icon ideas on Google and used the pen tool to trace and alter my inspiration. I especially enjoyed building my own color palettes for the stickers.
