The Coeur System

Context: Project for Design for America


Duration: 2 Quarters (Fall 2017 - Winter 2018)


Collaboration: Project Lead of 5-person team


Tools: Adobe Illustrator

I was the project lead of this project that was part of the Design for America club at the University of Washington. Our project focused on how to help high school students through panic attacks.
