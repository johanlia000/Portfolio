The Coeur System

Context: Project for Design for America


Duration: 2 Quarters (Fall 2017 - Winter 2018)


Collaboration: Project Lead of 5-person team


Tools: Adobe Illustrator

As the project lead of this Design for America at Univesrity of Washington (DFA) project I led a 5-person interdisciplinary team in the design of an original, wearable technology prototype that helps high school students relax during a panic attack at school. Through this project I practiced my leadership, teamwork, and human centered design skills.

OUR TEAM





Our interdisciplinary team consisted of Human Centered Design and Engineering, business, and industrial design majors ranging freshman to seniors. Through this project I learned that for me teamwork includes friendship. This creates a team dymamic that is welcoming and safe for creative, outside the box ideas.

Our whole team is passionate about design, technology, and designing for social good and brought different persepctives and interests to the design process with our diverse backgrounds. This was one of my first design group projects, and it made me realize that I love designing with a team and that I want to further puruse design projects.
